Meet Your Guide to Inner Peace

Hi, I'm Nikhil, a certified Yoga Instructor and passionate wellness guide dedicated to helping individuals discover strength, balance, and inner peace through yoga.

With a strong foundation in yoga science and traditional practices, I completed my training from Monargi Deshi National Institute of Yoga. I hold a Foundation Course in Yoga, CCYPI Certification, Diploma in Yoga Science, and specialized training in Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga.

Over the years, I have guided students of all levels — from beginners to advanced practitioners — in improving flexibility, posture, mindfulness, and overall well-being through structured and mindful practice.

My philosophy is simple: Yoga is not about perfection; it’s about progress and self-awareness. Every pose, every breath, and every session is an opportunity to grow physically, mentally, and spiritually.

Whether you're looking to:

  • Improve flexibility and strength
  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Learn Ashtanga Vinyasa flow
  • Start your yoga journey from basics

I provide a safe, supportive, and growth-focused environment where transformation happens naturally.
Join me and begin your journey toward a healthier, stronger, and more balanced life.
